Dapur omre

Description

A fun chicken recipe the kids can help prepare. They love crushing the crackers. It does not matter if the measurements aren't perfect, just wing it!

Ingredients

  • Ayam
  • Tepung
  • Bumbu Racik

Steps

  1. Marinasi ayamnya
  2. Baluri tepung ayamnya
  3. Goreng dengan minyak panas
  4. Tiriskan jika sudah bewarna golden brown
